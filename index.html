FAQs

What is "NoFakes"?

"NoFakes" is an browser extension that warns you about the accuracy of a site by the reports it received.

How does "NoFakes" work?

When you enter a site that received over (number) reports, a pop-up notification will warn you by showing the number of reports the site received.

What do the creators of the extension aim to achieve?

We want to provide you with the most accurate information.

Why does it matter if the information is real or fake?

We mostly make decisions based on information. If the information we know is fake it can affect our decision(s) in a bad way.
